So, there are many scoreboards and some tags and bossbars in the customcrafting section, this is a place where these things are described:


Scoreboards:

All scoreboards beginn with "cc." this phrase is not listed in here.

- "remove1" Used for subtract things in the crafting field.

- "slotCount" Used to test for the slots in the crafting field.

- "ccrafting" Used for making the crafting possible.

Tags:

Same as scoreboards.

- "customcrafting" Used for idendtify entitys that are used for the crafting table.


Bossbars:


NBT:

- "{cc:1b}" This NBT-Tag is to identify the glass panes in the crafting fiel.
